Our Work

From 2023 to 2028, the SRH #BIC Movement continues to unite communities, faith networks, and volunteers to promote moral culture change that safeguards every child. Below are highlights of our ongoing initiatives:

1. Age of Consent Petition

Advocating for policy that protects children through raising awareness, education, and moral reform.

2. SRH #BIC Training

Empowering leaders and community members through structured training on SRH #BIC principles.

3. Community Booths with Ready Set Go Sports Ministry

Using sports as a platform to promote morality, teamwork, and youth engagement across communities.

4. Talent Show & Media

Providing creative spaces for young people to express their values and talents through moral storytelling, art, and media.

5. Evangelism, Daily Bible Reading & Prayer

Promoting spiritual growth and daily moral grounding among volunteers and families.

Developing national moral policy frameworks that place the Best Interest of the Child at the center of every SRH decision.
